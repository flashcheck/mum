Our Story

USDT Check was founded in 2023 by a team of blockchain security experts with a mission to make cryptocurrency safer for everyone. We recognized that as Tether (USDT) became one of the most widely used stablecoins in the world, the need for reliable verification tools grew exponentially.

Our platform leverages advanced blockchain analytics and machine learning algorithms to provide comprehensive security assessments of USDT wallets. We analyze transaction patterns, check for known vulnerabilities, and check the legitimacy of wallets to help users protect their assets from scams and fraudulent activities.

What started as a small research project has evolved into a comprehensive security platform trusted by thousands of users worldwide. Our commitment to innovation drives us to continuously enhance our algorithms and expand our detection capabilities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safer cryptocurrency ecosystem by providing accessible tools that empower users to check the security and legitimacy of USDT wallets before engaging in transactions.

Quick Answers

USDT Verify is a security tool that helps you check if your Tether (USDT) wallet is secure, legitimate, and free from suspicious activities. Our platform analyzes transaction patterns, checks for known vulnerabilities, and provides a comprehensive security assessment to help protect your digital assets.

Our verification process involves analyzing your wallet address using advanced blockchain analytics. We check transaction patterns, assess connections to known scam addresses, verify contract interactions, and evaluate overall wallet health. Based on these factors, we generate a risk score and provide detailed insights about your wallet's security status.

Yes, we take privacy very seriously. All verifications are performed securely, and we do not store your wallet address or transaction data after the verification process is complete. Our system only accesses publicly available blockchain data to perform the security assessment.

The risk score is a numerical representation of your wallet's overall security status on a scale of 1-10, with 10 being the most secure. This score is calculated based on multiple factors including transaction history, connections to flagged addresses, contract interactions, and other security indicators.

While our system is designed to detect many common scam patterns and security vulnerabilities, no verification system can guarantee 100% detection of all possible scams, especially new or sophisticated ones. We continuously update our algorithms to improve detection capabilities, but we recommend using our tool as part of a broader security strategy.

We recommend verifying your wallet before engaging in significant transactions, after receiving funds from new sources, or if you notice any suspicious activity. Regular verification, such as monthly checks, can also help maintain ongoing awareness of your wallet's security status.

Currently, USDT Verify specializes in Tether (USDT) wallet verification. We're working on expanding our services to include other major cryptocurrencies and tokens in the future. Stay tuned for updates on our supported currencies.

If your wallet receives a low security score, review the detailed report for specific issues identified. Common recommendations include transferring funds to a new, secure wallet, reviewing recent transactions for unauthorized activity, updating security measures like two-factor authentication, and being cautious about future interactions. For serious security concerns, consider consulting with a blockchain security professional.
